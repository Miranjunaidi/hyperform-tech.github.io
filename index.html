About VISION

What is VISION?

VISION is a new-age smartphone manufacturing and design startup founded by passion and commitment to make India technologically self-reliant. The VISION smartphone will enable the people to experience all the best features of a smartphone along with the pride of a Make In India product.


Why VISION?

Despite the 1.3 billion population India doen't have a single successful flagship smartphone brand. Now more than ever the importance and emergence of digital devices are on the rise. We as a startup want to be the first powered by youth and noncorporate Indian smartphone manufacturers.


For whom is VISION?

With the change in consumer behavior of moving from imported products, our customer is everyone who now realizes the problem of not having something of their own. Organizations, Youth, Government, Industries everyone wants to make a switch to what they call "Desi".

Founders' Story

The founders are all young minds with immense technical, business and entrepreneurial skills with experience in various countries but they all share an immense love towards India. They are motivated individuals who work towards reducing the brain-drain from the country and increase employment, improve economy and make India self sufficient all over again! They have been successfully leading India's only Student-run Research Lab which has over 500 students and has won an accolade of the QS Silver Award in reimagining education after competing with student labs from the likes of MIT & Harvard.
